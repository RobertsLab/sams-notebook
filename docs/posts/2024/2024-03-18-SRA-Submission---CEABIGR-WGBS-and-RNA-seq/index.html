Finally got around to getting the CEABIGR (GitHub repo) sequencing data uploaded to NCBI’s Short Read Archive (SRA). Data transfer took quite awhile, as we have both whole genome bisulfite (WGBS) and RNA-seq data for this project. Once that was completed, I filled out the required metadata and completed the submission.

The data is now available in the SRA, under BioProject PRJNA1089235. Will get accessions added to Nightingales (Google Sheet).
