INTRO

I previously isolated RNA and needed to quantify it.

MATERIALS & METHODS

All samples were quantified using the Roberts Lab Qubit 3.0 with the broad range (BR) RNA Assay Kit.

Samples were measured using 1uL of sample.

Note

Some samples ended were too low concentration for the broad range assay using 1uL. They were re-measured using 5uL of sample with the broad range assay.
